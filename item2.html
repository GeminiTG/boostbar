Mint Chocolate

About the Flavor:

Mint Chocolate... ah, who doesn't love the refreshing taste of mint, right? This was our hardest bar to perfect, as many of our taste-testers kept telling us that it tasted like toothpaste. Now, we here at BoostBar are all for brushing your teeth - but we don't want our bars to taste like that. But we got there in the end, and the result is a delicious chocolatey bar that has plenty of that fresh mint flavor that's much more like the mint flavor in ice cream. We hope you like it as much as we do?

Well... considering that it is our second most popular flavor, you do clearly like it... but it's not for everyone and we get that.
